Services

Which Russian language related service do you need?

Translation

Translation of any type of content from English to Russian: webpages, apps, games, blogs, manuals, subtitles, etc.

Daily output depends from the type of your content and my current schedule. Generally, it's between 1k words and 3k words per day.

I use professional software like SDL Trados. Thorough proofreading and unlimited revisions are included to the price.

Editing

Editing of any type of Russian content: improving quality of provided content according to your style preferences, correcting grammar mistakes, typos.

Daily output depends on the type and quality of provided content.

Proofreading

Correcting basic mistakes of Russian content: grammar errors, typos, formatting.

Daily output is up to 10k words.

Transcribing

Transcribing of Russian audio/video content using prefered format.

Daily output is up to 1 hour of provided audio/video.

Copywriting

Writing Russian article on required topic using your style preferences and brief.

About Me

My Story

I started to offer language services in 2017 on Upwork as a side project. In 2020, I quit my main job as a private label category team lead and started my backpacking trip in Latin America that is still going - I'm travelling in Brasil right now. During this adventure, I learned Spanish and Portuguese.

Immersing myself into different cultures gave me understanding on how languages work, and what's important in interpreting information for people with different cultural background. I have NSUEM master degree in international trading, know business and marketing foundamentals, and have a decent experience in a negotiation with foreign partners. I know HTML/CSS/JS languages (this site is made by me) and can localize a web content that uses these languages directly.

Also, I write poems on Russian, play piano, harmonica - my creative side helps me to bring life in translated/edited content and make not just a dry translation but a piece of art.
